1)While stranding between two cable stations, hold the handles attached to the dumbbells of a high pulleys with an overhand grip.

2)Outstretch your arms while you do so. Bend your hips and knees such that you are leaning forward. But do not go too far.

3)Bend Your elbows and keep them slightly behind you. Curl your shoulders inwards towards your chest and bring the grip attachments together in front of your chest.

4)Once you start to feel a stretch in your chest muscles, begin to return to the initial position.

5)Repeat the above steps and complete your sets.
